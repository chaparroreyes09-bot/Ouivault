Security

Built from the Ground Up for Security

Every layer of the OuiVault stack is engineered to protect your assets — from key generation to long-term cold storage.

🌡️

Hybrid Custody Model

OuiVault uses a three-tier custody architecture. Active assets sit in a hot layer for fast access, medium-value holdings in a warm layer with multi-sig controls, and long-term storage in an air-gapped cold layer with time-lock withdrawals. The AI engine moves assets between tiers automatically based on your risk profile.

HOT WALLET WARM STORAGE COLD VAULT AI TIERING
🔐

Encryption Standards

All key material is encrypted using AES-256-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation (310,000 iterations, SHA-256). Seed phrases are never stored in plaintext. Client-side generation means your entropy never touches our infrastructure — verified by open-source code.

AES-256-GCM PBKDF2 BIP39 OPEN SOURCE
🧩

Sharded Key Management

The full OuiVault platform will implement Shamir's Secret Sharing (SSS) to split your private key into geographic shards stored across independent, air-gapped HSMs. No single party — including OuiVault — can reconstruct your key alone.

SHAMIR SSS HSM BACKED GEOGRAPHIC SPLIT
🏛️

Internal Controls

OuiVault maintains strict internal security controls including: mandatory 2-of-3 multi-sig for all cold withdrawals, biometric + hardware token authentication for staff access, quarterly third-party penetration tests, and a responsible disclosure bug bounty programme.

MULTI-SIG BIOMETRIC PEN TESTED BUG BOUNTY

About OuiVault

Built to Redefine Crypto Custody

OuiVault was founded by a team of cryptographers, security engineers, and fintech veterans with a shared conviction: that self-custody should not require a computer science degree, and that institutional-grade security should be accessible to everyone.

We started with a simple observation — most crypto wallets force users to choose between security and convenience. Hardware wallets are cumbersome. Software wallets are vulnerable. Custodial exchanges are single points of failure. OuiVault eliminates that trade-off entirely through a first-of-its-kind hybrid custody architecture: AI-tiered risk management, Shamir's Secret Sharing, and biometric-linked time-locked vaults — all in one seamless interface.

Our wallet generator, available today, is just the beginning. It demonstrates our commitment to transparency and client-side security — every key is generated locally in your browser, never transmitted, never stored. The full OuiVault custody platform launches Q4 2026.

Our Mission

To make institutional-grade crypto security available to every individual — without compromise on usability, privacy, or sovereignty.
