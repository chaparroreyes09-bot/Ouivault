About OuiVault

Bridging Traditional Finance and the Decentralised World

OuiVault is a premier crypto custodial wallet platform dedicated to securing digital assets for everyday users. We provide secure, user-friendly solutions for storing, transacting, and managing cryptocurrency — making financial sovereignty accessible to everyone, not just the technically fluent.

As we scale ahead of our Q4 2026 full platform launch, we're building the human layer that sets us apart — a network of dedicated Remote Support Agents and Relationship Officers who ensure every client feels supported at every step of their crypto journey.

Onboarding Programme

5-Step Agent Training Packet

Every new agent completes our structured 5-Step onboarding packet before going live. Each step builds on the last — by step 5 you'll be confidently handling real sessions.

S·1
Platform Orientation & Security Training
Tour of the OuiVault platform — wallet generator, supported chains, how the UI works from a client's perspective.
Introduction to the Support Centre — your primary work tool for all client communications.
How to log in as an agent, manage your queue, pick up conversations, and close sessions properly.
OuiVault security principles — what we never ask clients for (seed phrases, private keys), how to spot and report scam attempts.
Escalation protocol — when and how to flag issues to senior staff.
Note: All internal communications outside the support platform — HR queries, payroll, leave requests — go directly to hr@ouivault.com. Do not use the client support channel for internal matters.
S·2
Customer Service Best Practices
The OuiVault tone — warm, professional, never condescending. How to communicate complex crypto concepts simply.
Opening and closing a support session — greetings, setting expectations, professional sign-offs.
Handling frustrated or confused clients — de-escalation language, patience techniques, and when to transfer.
Response time standards — target reply times for chat vs email, what to say when you need a moment to investigate.
Documentation discipline — logging every interaction accurately in the platform, tagging appropriately.
Common client mistakes and how to address them without making the client feel embarrassed.
S·3
Crypto Basics — BTC, USDT & Wallets
What is a blockchain? How blocks, transactions, and confirmations work in plain English.
Bitcoin (BTC) — what it is, how it's sent, transaction fees, confirmation times, why it's first priority at OuiVault.
USDT (Tether) — stablecoins explained, difference between USDT on TRC-20 (Tron) vs ERC-20 (Ethereum), why network choice matters when sending.
Wallet types — hot vs cold, custodial vs non-custodial, what OuiVault generates and why it's safe.
Seed phrases and private keys — what they are, why clients must store them offline, what happens if they're lost.
Compatible wallets — Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, Rabby, Zerion, MetaMask, Phantom — how clients import OuiVault-generated wallets into these apps.
S·4
Transaction Processing Workflow
Step-by-step: how to guide a client through their first crypto purchase — which exchanges to recommend (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken) and why.
Network fees (gas fees) — what they are, why they fluctuate, how to advise clients on timing large transactions.
Sending and receiving — address verification, double-checking networks before sending, the irreversibility of blockchain transactions.
Common errors — wrong network, insufficient gas, pending transactions — how to explain and resolve each.
What to do when a transaction is stuck — mempool basics, replace-by-fee, when to tell the client to wait vs escalate.
Red flags — signs a client may be a target of a scam. Protocol for addressing this sensitively and reporting internally.
S·5
Live Support
Final onboarding sign-off — confirm platform access, verify documentation habits, confirm HR and escalation contacts are saved.
You're live — welcome to the OuiVault support team.
